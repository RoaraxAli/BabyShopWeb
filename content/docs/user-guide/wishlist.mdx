# Wishlist ️

The **Wishlist** feature lets you save products you're interested in and receive **automatic email notifications** the moment an out-of-stock product becomes available again.

---

## Adding to Your Wishlist

You can add any product to your wishlist from two places:

1. **Product Card** on the Home Screen — tap the **heart icon** on the card
2. **Product Details Screen** — tap the **heart icon** in the top-right corner

The heart icon fills with **pink** when the product is wishlisted, and returns to outline when removed. Tapping again toggles the wishlist state.

---

## Viewing Your Wishlist

From the **bottom navigation bar**, tap **"Wishlist"** (heart icon) to open your Wishlist Screen.

The Wishlist Screen shows all saved products with:
- Product image
- Name and category
- Price
- Current stock status
- Quick "Add to Cart" button (enabled when in stock)
- Remove button

---

## Stock Status in Wishlist

Each item shows its current stock level prominently:

| Status | Display |
|---|---|
| In stock | Green "In Stock" label |
| Low stock | Amber "Only N left" warning |
| Out of stock | Red "Out of Stock" label + "Notify Me when available" hint |

---

## Back-in-Stock Email Alerts 

This is one of BabyShopHub's most powerful features. Here's how it works:

1. You wishlist a product that is currently **out of stock**
2. Your email address is saved in the Firestore `wishlists` collection linked to that product
3. When an **admin restocks** the product (stock goes from 0 to any positive number), BabyShopHub automatically:
   - Queries all emails registered in the `wishlists` collection for that product
   - Sends each person a personalized **restock alert email**

### Restock Email Example

> **Subject:** Back in Stock: Wooden Stacking Activity Toy — BabyShopHub
>
> Good News! Wooden Stacking Activity Toy is Back in Stock  
> Price: $18.99  
> Only 12 items left in stock!

---

## Managing Wishlist Items

- **Remove from Wishlist**: Tap the heart icon again on either the wishlist screen or the product card/detail page
- **Add to Cart**: Tap "Add to Cart" directly from the Wishlist Screen when the item is in stock
- Removing an item from the wishlist also removes your email from the restock alert list for that product

---

## Notes

<Callout type="info">
Wishlist data is stored in Firestore (`wishlists` collection) and persists across sessions and devices. Your wishlist is tied to your account email.
</Callout>

<Callout type="warn">
If you are not logged in, wishlist actions are tracked locally only (with a fallback email of `anonymous_parent`). Log in to ensure your wishlist is synced and you receive restock alerts.
</Callout>
