# Product Details 

Tapping any product card opens the **Product Details Screen** — a rich, immersive page that gives you everything you need to make a confident purchase.

---

## Image Gallery

At the top of the product page is a full-width **image carousel**. Swipe left or right to browse multiple product photos. Tap any image to view it in full-screen mode.

Products can have multiple images uploaded by the admin (via Cloudinary). If only one image is available, the carousel shows a single image.

---

## Product Information

Below the gallery you'll find:

| Section | Content |
|---|---|
| **Category Badge** | Shows the product category (e.g. Clothing, Diapers) |
| **Product Name** | Full product title |
| **Rating Stars** | Average rating (e.g. ½) with total review count |
| **Price** | Listed price in USD |
| **Stock Status** | In Stock / Low Stock / Out of Stock |
| **Description** | Full product description with ingredients, materials, and safety notes |

---

## Stock Status Colors

- 🟢 **In Stock** — more than 5 units available
- 🟡 **Low Stock** — 5 or fewer units remaining (visible warning banner)
-  **Out of Stock** — 0 units; Add to Cart is disabled; "Notify Me" / Wishlist button is shown

---

## Adding to Cart

Tap the **"Add to Cart"** button at the bottom of the screen. If the item is already in your cart, tapping again increments the quantity (up to the available stock limit).

<Callout type="info">
You cannot add more items than what is currently in stock. The app enforces stock limits at both the UI and checkout levels.
</Callout>

---

## Wishlist from Product Page

Tap the **heart icon** in the top-right of the page to add/remove the product from your Wishlist. The icon fills pink when wishlisted.

If the product is out of stock, wishlisting it registers you for an automatic **back-in-stock email alert** (see [Wishlist](/docs/user-guide/wishlist)).

---

## Customer Reviews

Scroll down past the product info to see the **Reviews section**:

- **Overall rating bar** — Shows distribution of 1–5 star ratings as progress bars
- **Individual reviews** — Each review shows reviewer name, star rating, comment, and date

Reviews are seeded by admin and can be written by users (feature managed by the admin panel).
