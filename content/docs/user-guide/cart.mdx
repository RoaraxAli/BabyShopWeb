# Shopping Cart 

The **Cart Screen** is where you review your selected items, adjust quantities, apply discount codes, and see your order total before proceeding to checkout.

---

## Opening the Cart

Tap the **cart icon** in the top-right of the Home Screen. A badge shows the total number of items in your cart (counting quantities, not unique products).

---

## Cart Items

Each item in the cart shows:

- Product image thumbnail
- Product name and category
- Unit price
- Quantity selector (− / +)
- Line total (unit price × quantity)
- Remove button (trash icon)

---

## Adjusting Quantities

Tap **"+"** to increase quantity or **"−"** to decrease it.

- Minimum quantity is **1**. Tapping **"−"** at quantity 1 removes the item entirely.
- Maximum quantity is capped at the **current stock level** — you cannot order more units than are available.

---

## Removing Items

Tap the **trash/delete icon** next to an item to remove it from the cart entirely. You can also reduce quantity to 0 using the **"−"** button.

---

## Promo Codes

Below your cart items is a **Promo Code field**. Enter a code and tap **"Apply"**.

### Available Promo Codes

| Code | Discount | Minimum Order |
|---|---|---|
| `FIRSTBABY` | 15% off subtotal | None |
| `BABYSAVE10` | $10.00 flat off | $30.00 minimum |
| `NEWPARENT` | 20% off subtotal | None |

<Callout type="info">
Promo codes are case-insensitive. `firstbaby`, `FIRSTBABY`, and `FirstBaby` all work.
</Callout>

If a code requires a minimum order and your subtotal is below it, an error message is shown and the code is not applied.

### Removing a Promo Code

Tap the **"×"** button next to the applied code, or the "Remove" link, to clear the discount.

---

## Order Summary

At the bottom of the cart:

| Line | Description |
|---|---|
| **Subtotal** | Sum of all items (price × quantity) |
| **Discount** | Promo code savings (shown in green if applied) |
| **Total** | Subtotal minus discount — minimum $0.00 |

---

## Proceeding to Checkout

Tap **"Proceed to Checkout"** to move to the [Checkout Screen](/docs/user-guide/checkout). The cart must have at least one item.

<Callout type="warn">
Your cart is stored **in memory only** and is not persisted between app sessions. If you close the app, your cart will be empty when you reopen it. Save items to your [Wishlist](/docs/user-guide/wishlist) to remember them later.
</Callout>
