# User Guide ️

Welcome to the **BabyShopHub Parent Help Center**. This guide covers every feature you have access to in the app — from creating your account to tracking your orders and securing your profile.

---

## What Can You Do in BabyShopHub?

- **Browse** hundreds of premium baby products across 5 categories
- **Search** by name or description and filter by category in real time
- **Wishlist** items and receive automatic email alerts when out-of-stock products return
- **Add to Cart** and apply promo codes for instant discounts
- **Checkout** securely with saved delivery addresses
- **Track Orders** through 7 real-time status stages from Pending to Delivered
- **Chat with an AI assistant** for product advice, shipping info, and more
- **Secure your account** with Google Authenticator (TOTP) 2FA
- **Customize your profile** with a custom avatar or photo upload
