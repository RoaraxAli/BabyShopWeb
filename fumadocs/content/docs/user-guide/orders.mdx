# Orders & Tracking 📦

The **Order History** and **Order Tracking** screens let you see all your past and current orders, and follow each one through its delivery journey in real time.

---

## Accessing Order History

From the **Profile Screen**, tap **"My Orders"** or **"Order History"** to see all orders placed by your account.

---

## Order History Screen

The Order History screen lists all your orders, sorted newest first. Each order card shows:

- **Order ID** (abbreviated)
- **Order date**
- **Total amount**
- **Number of items**
- **Current status badge** (color-coded)

Tap any order card to open the full **Order Tracking Screen**.

---

## Order Tracking Screen

The Order Tracking Screen shows the complete journey of your order with a **visual progress timeline**:

### Order Statuses (in sequence)

| # | Status | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Pending** | Order placed, awaiting admin processing |
| 2 | **Processing** | Admin has confirmed the order |
| 3 | **Packed** | Items have been packed and prepared for dispatch |
| 4 | **Shipped** | Package has been handed to the carrier |
| 5 | **Out For Delivery** | Package is with the local delivery agent |
| 6 | **Delivered** | Package delivered to your address ✅ |
| — | **Cancelled** | Order was cancelled (see below) |

Each completed stage is shown with a checkmark and the exact **timestamp** when that status was set.

---

## Status History

Below the timeline, the full **status history** is shown — every status change with its timestamp and an optional note from the admin (e.g. "Shipped via FedEx — tracking #12345").

---

## Cancelled Orders

If an order is cancelled, it shows a red **"Cancelled"** badge. The status history will show when and at what stage the cancellation occurred.

To request a cancellation, contact support within 30 minutes of placing the order (see [FAQ](/docs/user-guide/faq)).

---

## Order Details Panel

The tracking screen also shows:

- Full itemized list (product name, quantity, price, image)
- Delivery address used at checkout
- Order total (with promo discount if applicable)
- Email address associated with the order

---

## Re-ordering

Currently, BabyShopHub does not support one-tap re-ordering. To re-purchase items from a previous order, return to the Home Screen, find the products, and add them to your cart.

---

## Notifications for Order Updates

When the admin updates your order status, you receive an **in-app notification** in the [Notification Center](/docs/user-guide/notifications). Check there for the latest updates on all your orders.
